About Henna by Anjali

Henna by Anjali is Mumbai's leading mehendi artist, renowned for creating breathtaking bridal henna designs that blend traditional artistry with contemporary elegance. With over 6 years of experience in the wedding industry, Anjali has become the preferred choice for discerning brides seeking exceptional mehendi artistry.

Specializing in intricate bridal mehendi, Arabic designs, and traditional Indian patterns, Anjali uses only premium quality henna paste that ensures deep, long-lasting color. Her expertise extends beyond bridal mehendi to include mehendi for all wedding functions including engagement ceremonies, sangeet nights, and pre-wedding celebrations.

Every design is carefully crafted to reflect the bride's personality and wedding theme. From delicate floral motifs to elaborate mandala patterns, from personalized couple portraits hidden within the design to meaningful symbols and names, Anjali creates mehendi art that tells your unique love story.
